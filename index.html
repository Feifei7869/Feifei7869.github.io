Research interests

Near-infrared II fluorescence imaging, Super-resolution imaging, Advanced biomedical imaging, Technologies have a broad and profound impact on biomedical applications.

NIR-II imaging

We are developing various NIR-II (1000-3000 nm) microscopes for deep tissue imaging with high spatiotemporal resolution.

Super-resolution imaging

We are exploring different mechanisms to overcome the optical diffraction limit for label-free and fluorescence imaging.

Biomedical applications

We are using the developed advanced technologies to solve biomedical problems.
